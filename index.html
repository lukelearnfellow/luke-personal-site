ABOUT ME

Here's a little more about me.

I am an aspiring Software Developer and entrepreneur studying Computer Science, all while also attending High School! In addition, I'm the Founder & CEO of Learn Fellow, an online student-to-student tutoring platform dedicated to making academic support both effective and affordable.

From a very young age, I’ve always been fascinated by computers. I specifically enjoy learning about Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Robotics. I was formerly a Research Assistant at Kent State University, and I continue to run my popular Newsletter and YouTube channel where I explore emerging technologies.

I am largely self-taught, having acquired proficiency in several programming languages and other software applications. In my free time, I enjoy writing articles—featured in my section—on topics such as technology, productivity, and finance.
